About Us

At Raunak Intermediaries LLP, we believe skincare is more than routine — it’s self-care. Our range of high-performance products, including rejuvenating serums, sulfate-free shampoos, gentle facewash, and deeply hydrating lotions, are carefully formulated to bring out your natural glow.

Who We Are

Raunak Intermediaries LLP is a modern skincare company dedicated to creating gentle yet effective products that promote healthy skin and confidence. Our formulas are crafted using nature-inspired ingredients and dermatological science to give you visible, lasting results.

Whether you're refreshing your skin with our facewash, nourishing it with our serums, or maintaining softness with our lotions — we’re here to help you love your skin every day.
